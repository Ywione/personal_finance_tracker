About the HSBC parser

HSBC Bermuda's PDF statements are scanned images with no text layer, so this app OCRs them in your browser (nothing is uploaded anywhere) and reconstructs transactions from the running balance shown on each line. It's designed to be self-correcting rather than perfect — always check the review table before saving, especially the first statement you try.

All data lives only in your own Google Drive (app-private folder). This page never sends your statements or transactions anywhere else.
